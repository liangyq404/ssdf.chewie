What exactly is Chewie?

Chewie is like upgraded bubble-gum, but more than that. It comes with more choice not only for the flavor but also the physical texture. The chewing experience that Chewie provides is based on a simulation of chewing real food, which is an old habit decaded in the past decades.

Who is Chewie for?

Chewie is designed to be suitable for all age groups and gender. Different chewer can get different benefits.

Kids use Chewie to exercise their teeth and tongue for a healthy development, young groups use chewie to reshape the curve of their masseter muscle, elderly group use chewie to maintain the health condition of their teeth...

All of the above are accomplished through a rather joyful chewing experience, instead of cold, hard dental treatment! Whatever oral health problems you're facing, or just want a relaxing thing to do while minding other business, Chewie is what you're looking for!
