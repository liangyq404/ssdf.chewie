What exactly is Chewie?

Chewie is like upgraded bubble-gum, but more than that. It comes with more choice not only for the flavor but also the physical texture. The chewing experience that Chewie provides is based on a simulation of chewing real food, which is an old habit decaded in the past decades.

Who is Chewie for?

Chewie is designed to be suitable for all age groups and gender. Different chewer can get different benefits.

Kids use Chewie to exercise their teeth and tongue for a healthy development, young groups use chewie to reshape the curve of their masseter muscle, elderly group use chewie to maintain the health condition of their teeth...

All of the above are accomplished through a rather joyful chewing experience, instead of cold, hard dental treatment! Whatever oral health problems you're facing, or just want a relaxing thing to do while minding other business, Chewie is what you're looking for!

What does Chewie do?

Basically, Chewie is supposed to stimulate the whole chewing system and exercise them, the benefit of this kind of exercise various from different group of people. Check our plans to find more about the magic about Chewie!

What is Chewie made of?

The ingredients of Chewie are mainly from natural extracts, with different formula for different tastes and textures. The base of the chewable piece is food grade silicone, which made Chewie 100% safe to chew. It also uses the similar formula for bubble-gum, so don't worry about swallowing it - you body is able to excrete it.

How old do I have to be to get my Chewie?

Chewie has different plans for different age groups, but we only recommend kids older than 6 to use Chewie. From the age of six, children will go through the tooth replacement stage, when they git rid of the baby teeth and the grinding teeth grow out. That's when we expect Chewie to make the most of it.
