Leading Technology

Over the years, Chew Lab have been developing a technology to modify the texture and flavor of food grade silicone, which has been the main material for chewable products, such as chewelries.

In 2109, the lab successfully developed a patented formula to set the softness, the technology also allow us to determine the flavor of the silicone, as well as determining how long does it take for the material to become completely blend.
