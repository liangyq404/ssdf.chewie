Something Chew...

Did you know that people used to have to chew the food before they swallow it? Back in the days when the full nutrition pill wasn't fully popularized, our teeth were born with a harder mission than just creating a bright smile: chewing our food to make it easier for digesting.

However, it wasn't all that bad to chew! Medical and Scientific archives recorded that people actually enjoyed the mouthful sensation that the chewing behavior creates. Sometimes, the chewing experience is even more delightful than the flavor of the food itself. Sensation like this adds to the overall experience of eating, making it more a cultural behavior than a survival one. Scientific studies have found that the mouthful sensation also contributes to our memory of eating, as well as the satisfaction level of living.

Apart from a pure fulfillment of mouthful, Chewie is also beneficial for your health.

In our time, The reduced usage of the entire chewing system, which includes teeth, tongue, the masseter muscle, the occlusal joints and so on,resulted in the degeneration of each part of the system.
